## Core Philosophy

### The Mood Leaf Ethics

Mood Leaf follows strict ethical principles that guide everything we do:

#### 1. Never Diagnose
We will **never** label you with mental health conditions. Instead of "you have anxiety disorder," we say "you've been mentioning feeling anxious lately." Only qualified professionals can diagnoseâ€”we're not that.

#### 2. Never Prescribe
We will **never** tell you to start, stop, or change medications. Those decisions are between you and your doctor.

#### 3. Always Tentative
We use language like "it seems," "I wonder if," and "you might notice" because only YOU truly know your experience. We never tell you how you feel.

#### 4. Anti-Dependency
Mood Leaf is designed to help you **not need it**. We actively encourage:
- Real-world connections over app conversations
- Building your own coping skills
- Trusting your own wisdom
- Stepping away when appropriate

Our success is when you say "I don't need this app anymore."

#### 5. Privacy First
Everything stays on your device. We have no servers. We literally cannot see your data.

#### 6. Encourage Professional Help
For persistent struggles, we always encourage seeking professional support. We're a companion, not a replacement for therapy.

## Journaling (Leaves)

### The Leaf Philosophy

In Mood Leaf, journal entries are called **Leaves**. Like leaves on a tree, they're meant to be releasedâ€”noticed, acknowledged, and let go.

**Journaling is noticing, not archiving.**

A leaf is a single thought, word, or feeling:
- Short, not long
- Released, not saved
- Let go, not held onto

This protects you from the trap of endless reviewing and rumination.

### Creating Entries

#### Text Entry
1. Tap the **+** button on the home screen
2. Write your thoughts
3. Tap **Save**

#### Voice Entry
1. Tap the **+** button
2. Tap the **microphone icon**
3. Speak your entry
4. Review and edit if needed
5. Tap **Save**

### What Happens When You Save

When you save a leaf, Mood Leaf automatically:
- **Detects mood** - Positive, negative, or neutral with intensity
- **Extracts topics** - People, events, themes you mentioned
- **Updates your Tree** - Adds to your life context for better conversations
- **Finds significance** - Marks important entries that contain life context

### Entry Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Mood Detection** | Color-coded (green = positive, red = negative, gray = neutral) |
| **Topic Tags** | People, emotions, activities, life areas |
| **Talk About This** | Start a conversation about a specific entry |
| **Search & Filter** | Find entries by content, mood, or date |

## Talking to Your Guide (The Sprout)

### How Conversation Works

Your guide (The Sprout) doesn't just respond to your current messageâ€”it knows your whole story. Every conversation draws from:

- **Your journal entries** - Recent ones with full text
- **Your life context** - People, events, patterns from months of journaling
- **Your psychological patterns** - How you think, cope, and process
- **Your health data** - Sleep, activity, heart rate (if enabled)
- **Your preferences** - Communication style, what helps you most

### The Conversation Approach

Your guide follows a consistent approach:

1. **VALIDATE** - Acknowledge your feelings first
2. **EXPLORE** - Ask clarifying questions if needed
3. **SUPPORT** - Offer perspective or techniques
4. **EMPOWER** - End with something that builds your autonomy

### Different Modes

| Mode | Trigger | What Happens |
|------|---------|--------------|
| **Venting** | "I just need to vent" | Pure validation, no problem-solving |
| **Advice** | "What should I do?" | Explores options while encouraging your wisdom |
| **Processing** | Complex feelings | Gentle questions to help you understand |
| **Celebrating** | Sharing wins | Genuine celebration without overdoing it |

### What Your Guide Knows

Your guide has access to **all your data** with exact timestamps:

| Data Type | What It Knows | Example Questions |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Tracking Logs** | Every tap with exact counts | "How many times did I exercise this month?" |
| **Journal Entries** | Recent entries with full text | "What did I write about yesterday?" |
| **Lifestyle Factors** | Caffeine, sleep, outdoor time | "How much coffee have I had this week?" |
| **Health Data** | Heart rate, sleep, activity | "How did I sleep last night?" |
| **Life Context** | People, milestones, themes | "When did I start therapy?" |
| **Current Time** | Exact date and time | "How does today compare to last week?" |

### Crisis Handling

If you mention self-harm, suicide, or crisis:
1. Mood Leaf immediately provides crisis resources
2. **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** (call or text)
3. **Crisis Text Line** (text HOME to 741741)
4. This happens BEFORE any AI processing

## Quick Logs (Twigs/Branches)

### What Are Twigs?

Twigs (also called Branches) are one-tap buttons for tracking anything:
- Habits you're building
- Habits you're breaking
- Medications
- Symptoms
- Goals
- Anything you want to count

### Branch Types

| Type | Example | Goal |
|------|---------|------|
| **Habit Build** | "Meditated" | Do MORE of this |
| **Habit Break** | "No alcohol" | Do LESS of this |
| **Medication** | "Morning meds" | Consistency |
| **Symptom** | "Anxious" | Awareness |
| **Goal** | "Applied for job" | Milestone |
| **Custom** | Anything | User-defined |

### What Your Guide Sees

For each twig, your guide sees:

```
ðŸƒ Exercised:
    - Today: 2 times (at 7:30 AM, 5:15 PM)
    - This week: 8 times
    - This month: 25 times
    - All time: 47 times
    - Current streak: 5 days
    - Longest streak: 12 days
    - First logged: November 15, 2025
    - Last logged: Today at 5:15 PM
```

This means real answers based on your actual data.

### Why Branches Matter

Unlike leaves (which fall), branches stay and grow:
- **Streaks show growth** - But missing days don't "break" anything
- **No punishment** - The tree is still there even if you skip
- **Pattern visibility** - See what you're building over time

## Health Integration (Weather)

### Why Connect Health Data?

Your body often knows before your mind does. Heart rate spikes, poor sleep, and low activity all affect how you feel. Connecting HealthKit lets your guide:

- Notice when your heart rate spikes (stress indicator)
- Acknowledge poor sleep might affect your mood
- Point out patterns ("you feel better on active days")
- Send gentle check-ins when your body signals stress

### Available Health Data

| Data Type | What's Tracked | How It Helps |
|-----------|----------------|--------------|
| **Heart Rate** | Current, resting, spikes | Detects stress before you notice |
| **HRV** | Heart rate variability | Measures nervous system stress |
| **Sleep** | Duration, quality, awakenings | Correlates with mood |
| **Activity** | Steps, exercise minutes | Connects movement to wellbeing |

### Heart Rate Spike Detection

When your heart rate spikes significantly:
1. Your phone buzzes with a gentle notification
2. "Your heart rate is up. What's going on?"
3. Tap to journal about it
4. Connect physical sensations to emotions

Notifications are rate-limited (max 1 per 30 minutes).

### Sleep-Mood Correlation

Over 90 days, Mood Leaf tracks:
- Your mood scores on good sleep nights
- Your mood scores on poor sleep nights
- Whether YOU specifically feel better with more sleep

**Example insight:**
> "Looking at your data, you consistently feel better on days after 7+ hours of sleep."

### Activity Correlation

Similarly tracks:
- Mood on active days vs. sedentary days
- Whether movement improves YOUR wellbeing

## Privacy & Your Data

### Where Your Data Lives

**Everything stays on your device.** Mood Leaf uses local storage which means:

| Data Type | Location |
|-----------|----------|
| Journal entries | Your device only |
| Life context | Your device only |
| Chat history | Your device only |
| Health data | Your device only |
| Settings | Your device only |
| API key | Secure keychain |

**We have no servers.** We cannot see your data.

### What's Sent to AI

When you chat, we send:
- Your current message
- Recent conversation (last ~6 messages)
- A **compressed summary** of your life context (not raw journals)
- Current health snapshot (if enabled)

**We never send:**
- Full text of all journal entries
- Complete chat history
- Raw health data dumps

### Secure Delete

For extra peace of mind:
1. **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Secure Delete**
2. Enter confirmation code
3. Data is overwritten 3 times before deletion
4. Makes recovery virtually impossible

### Your Rights

You can always:
- Export all your data (JSON format)
- Delete all your data
- Disable any feature
- Revoke HealthKit access
- Remove your API key

## FAQ

### General

**Is Mood Leaf a replacement for therapy?**
No. Mood Leaf is a journaling companion. For serious or persistent struggles, please seek professional help.

**How much does it cost?**
The app is free. AI chat requires an Anthropic API key (~$2-5/month for regular use).

**Does it work offline?**
Journaling works offline. Chat requires internet.

### Privacy

**Can you see my data?**
No. We have no servers. Your data never leaves your device.

**What happens if I delete the app?**
All data is permanently deleted. Export first if you want to keep it.

### AI Guide

**Why does my guide use tentative language?**
Because only YOU know your experience. We say "it seems" and "I wonder if" to leave room for your truth.

**Can I change my guide's personality?**
Yes! Settings > Coach Settings > Choose Persona.

### Health

**Does Mood Leaf diagnose health issues?**
Never. We describe what we observe ("heart rate elevated"), never diagnose ("panic attack").

**Is HealthKit required?**
No. Mood Leaf works great without it.
