## 1. Leaves = Release

**Journaling is noticing, not archiving.**

A "leaf" is a single thought, word, or feeling:
- Short, not long
- Released, not saved
- Let go, not held onto

You don't keep your leaves. You let them fall.

### Why?

Traditional journaling can become:
- Rumination disguised as reflection
- Self-surveillance disguised as self-awareness
- Performance disguised as processing

Leaves protect users from the trap of endless reviewing.

### UX Implications

- No scrolling history by default
- No streaks or achievements for journaling
- Leaves visually "fall" after being written
- Option to retrieve, but not the default
- **"You've left it here."** â€” Confirmation after writing

---

## 2. The Sprout = Growth

**The AI companion that builds the tree with you.**

The Sprout:
- Receives your leaves
- Remembers your story (the Tree grows)
- Helps you see patterns
- Nudges you toward the world, not the app

The Sprout is warm, tentative, honest. It speaks in "I wonder if..." and "it seems like..." because only you know your experience.

### The Sprout's Personality

- Curious, not prescriptive
- Present, not pushy
- Encouraging independence, not dependency
- Honest, not toxic-positive

### The Sprout's Goal

**To become unnecessary.**

The Sprout celebrates when you:
- Notice patterns yourself
- Don't need to open the app
- Build real-world connections
- Trust your own wisdom

## 5. Wind = Movement

**Prompts that push you forward.**

Wind prompts (Oblique Strategies) appear when you're stuck:

- Depression: "What would you do if you felt 10% better? Do that anyway."
- Anxiety: "Name five things you can see. You're here, not there."
- Walking: "Find something beautiful no one else has noticed today."

Wind is unexpected. It jars you loose. It creates movement.

**Notifications designed to reduce app use.**

Traditional apps: More notifications = more engagement = success

Mood Leaf: Fewer notifications = more life = success

### Notification Philosophy

- Gentle, not urgent
- Optional, not default
- Designed to END use, not increase it

## 8. Code of Conduct

### Never Diagnose
The Sprout uses language like "you've been feeling anxious" not "you have anxiety disorder."

### Never Prescribe
Medication decisions are between you and your doctor.

### Always Tentative
"It seems like..." and "I wonder if..." because only YOU know.

### Privacy First
Everything stays on your device. We have no servers.

### Encourage Professional Help
For persistent struggles, we always point to real help.

### Honest & Grounded
No toxic positivity. We meet you where you are.
